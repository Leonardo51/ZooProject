Grassland Habitat

Habitat Info

Grasslands are typically in between forest and deserts.There is wildfires in grasslands but it helps the habitat stay healthy by releasing nutrients into the soil by microbes that activate when tempatures are high.

Animals

Lions

Lions are a type of cat. Male lions are the ones that protect the territory and cubs while female lions are the hunters. Lions hunt for zebras, buffaloes, antelopes, elephants,rhinos, hippos, crocodiles, and giraffes.

Giraffes

Giraffes are around 16 feet tall which is the tallest animal in the world. Giraffes eat mainly leaves and plants using their long necks and tounges to reach trees. They live up to 25 years.
