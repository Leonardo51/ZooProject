Ocean Habitat

Habitat Info

There are a 5 total oceans which are the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Artic ocean. In the ocean there are more habitats specfic for certain animals. Some of the habitats are coral reefs habitat, kelp forest habitats.

Animals

Fin Whale

Fin whales are able to live from 80 to 90 years and are 75 to 85 feet long. They can also weigh 40 to 80 tons. They have v shaped heads with a skinny body. Fin whales usually eat small schools of fish or squids.

Green Sea Turtle

Green sea turtles are about 3-4 feet long and weigh 240 to 420 pounds.They are herbivores so they mainly eat algae and sea grass. When they lay eggs they lay about 150 eggs in every nest.
