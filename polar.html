Polar Habitat

Habitat Info

Polar habitats are located at the north and south parts of the Earth.There is no physical ground there is floating ice. Plants are unable to grow in these areas but during summer there are small patches of moss.

Animals

Polar Bears

Polar bears are 7 to 8 feet and weigh about 1700 pounds. They can live 20 to 30 years. Their diet consists ringed or bearded seals, walruses, and seal pups.

Ringed Seals

Ringed seals live 25 to 30 years and are about 4 feet and weigh about 120 pounds. They mostly eat cod fish. Ringed Seals are the most common and smallest animal in the polar habitat.
