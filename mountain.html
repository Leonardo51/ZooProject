Mountain Habitat

Habitat Info

Mountains are in every continent of the world. There are many biomes on a mountain. At the bottom there are deciduous forests and at the top are pine forests. It gets cold the higher you go up a mountain.

Animals

Brown Bear

Brown bears live up to 25 years and can be 5 to 8 feet big. They have a great sense of smell and are able to run at 35mph. In summer they eat a lot so they can live off body fat in winter. Brown bears are omnivores that means they will eat anything.

Mountain Goat

Mountatin goats can live 9 to 15 years and are about 3.5 feet. They are herbivores so they only eat plants. Both male and female goats have a small beard and horns that are about 300 mm.
