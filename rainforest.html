Rainforest Habitat

Habitat Info

Rainforest are warm and wet habitats.The trees are very tall because they compete with other plants for sunlight.There are 4 layers of a forest wbich are emergent, canopy, understory, forest floor.

Animals

Spider Monkey

Spider-Monkeys live up to 25 years 1 to 2 feet. They have very long arms that are used to swing in trees. They are omnivores so they mainly eat plants.

Glass Frogs

Glass frogs are very small and grow up to 1 inch. Male frogs are very protective of thier partners eggs.There is not much knowledge about glass frogs.
